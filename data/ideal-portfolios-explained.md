# Ideal Portfolios — Stocks, Returns & Why Each Was Picked

Five model portfolios, one per life-stage scenario. Every stock clears two hard filters first: **total return June 2021 → June 2026 beats Nifty 50 (+53.7%)** AND **sound June-2021 fundamentals** (strong ROE, manageable debt, positive operating cash flow, consistent EPS, no governance flag). Within that eligible pool, each scenario picks by its own risk profile. Stock repetition across scenarios is intentional — the best eligible fit is reused rather than swapped for variety.

**Benchmark to beat: Nifty 50 = +53.7%.** All five portfolios beat it.
